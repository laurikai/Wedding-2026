FAQ

How do I get there?

The venue is located near Avignon in the South of France. Here are the best routes:

From Houston

  • Fly direct to Paris CDG + Train: Air France offers direct flights from Houston (IAH) to Paris CDG (~10 hours). Then take the TGV high-speed train to Avignon (2h 40min, trains depart directly from CDG or Paris Gare de Lyon).
  • Fly to Paris + Connect to Marseille or Lyon: From Paris, you can also connect to Marseille (1 hour from venue) or Lyon (1-hour TGV to Avignon).

From Tallinn

  • Direct to Nice + Train: airBaltic flies direct to Nice. From Nice, take the train to Avignon (~3 hours).
  • Direct to Paris CDG + Train: airBaltic and Air France fly direct to Paris (~3 hours). Then take the TGV to Avignon (2h 40min).
  • Via Paris to Marseille or Lyon: From Paris, you can also connect to Marseille (1 hour from venue) or Lyon (1-hour TGV to Avignon).

From Avignon TGV station, the château is just a 20-minute taxi ride away.

Book trains at SNCF Connect.

What should I wear?

We suggest semi-formal / garden party attire. Think elegant but comfortable - you'll be enjoying cocktails on the lawn with a live band before moving indoors for dinner and dancing.

Ladies, please note that parts of the venue have grass and gravel paths, so consider wedges or block heels rather than stilettos. Gentlemen, please wear a suit jacket.

Evenings can be cooler, so we recommend bringing a light layer or wrap.

What will the weather be like?

Late September in Provence is typically lovely - warm sunny days averaging around 24°C (75°F) with cooler evenings dropping to around 14°C (57°F).

Packing tips:

  • Light layers for temperature changes between day and evening
  • Comfortable shoes for outdoor spaces
  • Sunglasses and sunscreen for daytime activities
  • A light jacket or wrap for evenings
  • Swimwear - the farewell brunch will be by the pool and we welcome guests to take a dip

What amenities are on-site?

The château offers wonderful amenities for you to enjoy during your stay:

  • Two heated swimming pools - perfect for relaxing before or after the festivities
  • Outdoor hot tub - seats 7 people
  • Tennis court - the venue has rackets available, but feel free to bring your own
  • Beautiful gardens - manicured lawns, vine terraces, and pine-lined paths to explore

Feel free to make the most of these during your stay.

Places to explore before or after the wedding

Provence and the South of France offer so much to explore. Some highlights near the venue:

  • Avignon (20 min) - The famous Palais des Papes, Pont d'Avignon, and charming old town
  • Châteauneuf-du-Pape (15 min) - World-renowned wine region with tastings and vineyard tours
  • Luberon villages (45 min) - Picturesque hilltop villages like Gordes, Roussillon, and Ménerbes
  • Arles (40 min) - Roman ruins and Van Gogh's beloved landscapes
  • Marseille (1 hour) - Vibrant port city with excellent seafood and the stunning Calanques
  • Nice & Cannes (2.5 hours) - The glamorous French Riviera with beautiful beaches and the famous Promenade des Anglais
